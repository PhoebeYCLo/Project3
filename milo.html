MILO. is a online company that sell modern furniture.

In this project, I worked with team of 2. Our mission is be an UX & Web designer on this website.

Stage 1 - style guide

During the first stage, we were going to choose a idea to work on as a team. And created a style guide that would show the theme and overall style of the company.

Stage 2 - Structure

In this week, we started to build a structure with consistency. And also, we could fill real content in it. After we built initial set of our website, we link all pages together. I was mainly working on shop, about and shopping cart pages and make the website responsive. Also, I found images for our website.

Stage 3 - Developing

Keeping from last week, we were supposed to fill in all real content. Images and icons were supposed to be filled as well. This a week, a full functional website should be prepared. I finalized our images and button styles to make them look more consistent.
